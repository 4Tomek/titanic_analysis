Columns description:¶

PassengerId - passenger unique identifier
Survived - if passenger survived (1) or died (0)
Pclass - passenger class: 1 (luxury), 2 (middle), 3 (low)
Name - passenger name
Sex - male or female
Age - passenger age (float)
SibSp - sum of siblings and spoused traveling with the passenger
Parch - sum of parents and children traveling with the passenger
Ticket - passenger's ticket number
Fare - money spent on the ticket
Cabin - cabin category or number assigned to the passenger (if available)
Embarked - destination passenger embarked from (S=Southampton, C=Cherbourg, Q=Queenstown)
